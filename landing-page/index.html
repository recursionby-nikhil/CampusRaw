Your professor's account

Answering the obvious ones

Real questions.
Actual answers.

Will my parents be able to see this?+
Yes — content is public. That's kind of the point. Post responsibly. Or don't. We're not your RA.
What counts as "unfiltered"? Can I post anything?+
Unfiltered means real and raw — not illegal or harmful. There are basic community guidelines (no harassment, no dangerous content). But we're not here to police your vibe. Campus chaos is encouraged.
My university doesn't have a .edu email. Can I still join?+
Absolutely. We support international university domains — .ac.in, .ac.uk, .edu.au, and more. If it ends in something that screams "student", you're probably good. We'll have manual verification fallback too.
Is this free? What's the business model?+
Free to use. We're figuring out a creator-first monetization model that doesn't involve taking half your earnings or selling your attention to advertisers. More on that at launch.
When does this actually launch?+
We're targeting a beta in a few months, starting with select universities. Join the waitlist and you'll hear first — plus get to influence what we build next. You're basically a co-founder at this point.
Can I suggest my university for the first batch?+
Yes — we'll prioritize campuses with the most signups. So tell your friends. The more students from your uni on the list, the faster you all get access.
